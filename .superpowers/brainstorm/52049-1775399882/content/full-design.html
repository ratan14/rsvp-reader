Design Summary

📱 Portrait

  • Word display centered, maximized
  • Info bar hidden, tap word area to reveal
  • Transport buttons below progress bar
  • Horizontal joystick below transport
  • WPM value shown below joystick

📱 Landscape

  • Word display takes left area
  • 120px control panel on right
  • WPM value at top of panel
  • Vertical joystick in middle
  • Transport buttons at bottom

Joystick Behavior

Orientation: Vertical in landscape (thumb up/down), horizontal in portrait (thumb left/right)
At center: No change
Gentle push: ~5 WPM/sec
Half push: ~25 WPM/sec
Full push: 50 WPM/sec (max)
Release: Springs back to center, WPM holds
Range: 100–1000 WPM (clamped)
