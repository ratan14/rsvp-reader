D. Rate Joystick ★ NEW

Spring-loaded stick resting at center (neutral). Push up → WPM increases, push down → WPM decreases. The farther from center, the faster the rate of change. Release → snaps back to center, WPM holds at current value. Like a jog shuttle on video editors.

How the Joystick Works

At center: No change, WPM stays put
Slight push up: +5 WPM/sec
Half push up: +25 WPM/sec
Full push up: +100 WPM/sec
Release: Springs back to center
Benefit: One gesture covers both coarse and fine control
Metaphor: Like a jog shuttle wheel on video editing hardware
Thumb motion: Natural up/down in landscape right-hand grip
