Landscape Behavior

1

Right-Side Control Panel

Controls relocate from below the word to a ~140px column on the right. Word display gets full remaining width for maximum readability.

2

WPM Dial

Circular dial with arc indicator showing position in 100-1000 range. Rotate gesture to adjust — natural thumb motion when holding phone in landscape with right hand. WPM value shown large in center.

3

Transport Below Dial

Play/pause and skip buttons arranged horizontally beneath the dial. All within thumb reach in the right-side panel.

Portrait vs Landscape Summary

📱 Portrait

  • Controls below word
  • WPM shown as number
  • +/- buttons for WPM
  • Large circular transport

📱 Landscape

  • Controls on right column
  • WPM rotary dial
  • Transport below dial
  • Max word display width
